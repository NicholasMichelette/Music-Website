About us
What is Sounds Good?
Sounds Good is a cutting edge new tool that employs the tech industry's strongest new technologies to allow users to find totally new music that Sounds Good to them. By utilizing the latest analytical methods, Sounds Good can analyze each user's individual preferences and provide music suggestions totally personalized just for you! For registered users, each and every suggestion is hand-picked by our advanced, cutting-edge, industry-revolutioning systems that keep your likes and dislikes and preferences in mind. As you like and dislike songs, the results that show up for you become more and more custom tailored, ensuring that each user will always recieve only the most relevant song suggestions for them. More than that, Sounds Good also allows you, the user, to share your brilliant music taste with others, create playlists, follow the music tastes of others, and more!
Who are we?
The Sounds Good team is made up entirely of fresh developers breaking out into the industry with a strong background in Computer Science gained through rigorous and thorough studies at Rowan University, based right in New Jersey. The development team is made up of remote collaborators who know how to keep a user's quality of experience and ease of use in mind.
What Data do we Collect?
At Sounds Good, we understand that user privacy is of utmost importance, and as such, we use zero cookies or trackers here at Sounds Good. Instead, the data that we track consists of only each user's account's information, and each user's history of music preferences to make the magic happen. We don't collect or need anything else, allowing users to browse and find brand new music to them without any worries about their data.
The Authors
